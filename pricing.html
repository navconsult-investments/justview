CIN Fund Administration prides itself on its ability to provide high-touch service coupled with cost-effective pricing. Rated #1 Value for Money by HFM Insights, our fund administration pricing is straightforward. We start out by working with you to understand your administrative needs. Since an emerging cryptocurrency fund may have very different administrative needs than an established venture capital fund, we’ll ask relevant questions about your fund’s AUM, its complexity and the number of current investors. Unlike other administrators, we don’t add on unnecessary extra fees.
